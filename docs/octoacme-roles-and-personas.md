# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user flows, prototypes, and UI elements that ensure products are intuitive and user-friendly. They collaborate closely with Product Managers and Developers to align design with user needs and technical feasibility.

### Responsibilities
- Design user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and UI elements
- Collaborate with Product Managers to refine requirements and validate designs
- Review builds and features with Developers to ensure design fidelity
- Conduct usability testing and gather user feedback
- Provide feedback in sprint reviews and retrospectives

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and visually consistent experiences
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction
- Ensure design decisions are data-informed

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and handoff sessions with Developers
- Collaboration with Product Managers on requirements and user research
- Sprint demos and retrospective feedback

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers maintain CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and deployment automation. They ensure reliable, repeatable releases and collaborate with Developers and Project Managers to support smooth delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Set up and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure, environments, and automation for deployments
- Work closely with Developers on environment parity and tooling
- Support Project Managers for smooth release execution
- Monitor system health and respond to infrastructure incidents

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize downtime and infrastructure-related blockers
- Improve observability and automation across the delivery pipeline

### Typical Communication
- Release coordination with Project Managers
- Infrastructure and pipeline updates to Developers
- Incident response and post-mortems

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads coordinate test strategy, test automation, and quality gates. They partner with Developers and Product Managers to ensure features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Craft end-to-end test strategy and drive automation implementation
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Engage with Developers to improve test coverage
- Partner with Product Managers to validate features against acceptance criteria
- Define and enforce quality gates for releases

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality, reliable software releases
- Increase test coverage and reduce regression risk
- Enable fast feedback loops through automation

### Typical Communication
- Test planning and review sessions with Developers
- Acceptance criteria validation with Product Managers
- Quality gate sign-off for releases

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers ensure documentation quality by capturing processes, features, and release information. They work with all roles to keep documentation accurate, up-to-date, and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Document processes, features, and release notes
- Develop onboarding materials and user guides
- Solicit feedback from SMEs and Developers for accuracy
- Ensure docs are maintained, versioned, and discoverable

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and accessible documentation
- Reduce onboarding time and knowledge silos
- Support self-service troubleshooting and learning

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with SMEs and Developers
- Release note coordination with Product and Project Managers
- Onboarding and training sessions

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove blockers, and coach the team on Agile practices. They enable the team to focus on delivery and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Shield team from distractions and enable focus
- Track impediments and work with Project Managers to resolve blockers
- Coach the team on Agile and iterative practices
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and morale
- Ensure Agile ceremonies are effective and time-efficient
- Remove blockers and reduce process friction

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- Impediment escalation and resolution with Project Managers
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking
